# Data Visualization and Dashboarding (MCO461-1)

### Master of Commerce (Semester I)

### Total Duration: 45 Hours

## Module 1: Foundations of Data Visualization and Power BI (10 Hours)

### 1.1 Introduction to Data Visualization (2 Hours)

* Evolution and importance of data visualization
* Role of visualization in business decision-making
* Data-driven culture and analytics
* Visualization use cases across industries

### 1.2 Principles of Effective Visual Design (2 Hours)

* Visual perception and cognitive principles
* Data-ink ratio and chart effectiveness
* Avoiding misleading visualizations
* Best practices for dashboard design

### 1.3 Selecting the Right Visualization (2 Hours)

* Comparison, composition, distribution, and relationship charts
* Choosing appropriate chart types
* Common visualization mistakes
* Visualization storytelling basics

### 1.4 Introduction to Microsoft Power BI (2 Hours)

* Overview of Business Intelligence (BI)
* Power BI ecosystem and architecture
* Power BI Desktop, Service, and Mobile
* Components of Power BI

### 1.5 Understanding the Power BI Data Pipeline (2 Hours)

* Data sources and connectivity
* Data acquisition process
* ETL concepts in Power BI
* End-to-end reporting workflow

**Hands-on Lab:** Exploring Power BI Interface and Sample Reports
